# Code of Conduct

opa-authzen-plugin follows the [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md).

## Summary

The CNCF community is made up of people from many different backgrounds and experiences. We are committed to creating an open, inclusive, and welcoming community where all people feel comfortable and safe participating.

Our community operates under these core values:

- **Be inclusive**: We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences
- **Be respectful**: Treat others with kindness and respect
- **Be collaborative**: Work together constructively to achieve common goals

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers at:

- kanywst12@gmail.com

All reports will be handled with confidentiality and appropriate action will be taken.

For more details, please see the [full CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md).
